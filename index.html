My Philosophy

Code-First, Fail-Fast, Debug-Live

I believe reading theory in isolation has limitations. My approach is code-first: build something real, run into runtime exceptions, and debug them as I go. That's how concepts stick, and that's how I build strong engineering instincts.

This hands-on methodology has guided my computer science studies at the University of Westminster. Whether learning a language from scratch or configuring database schemas, I write code to understand it.

Now, I'm applying this same mindset to pivot into **Deep Learning and AI**. Rather than skipping straight to pre-built high-level models, I'm building my foundational understanding of network architectures, matrix operations, and optimization algorithms step by step.
